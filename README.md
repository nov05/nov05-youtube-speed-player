### 👉 Alternative: **Video Speed Controller**

    https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/video-speed-controller/nffaoalbilbmmfgbnbgppjihopabppdk

    Video Speed Controller is a lightweight **Chrome extension** that gives you full control over video playback speed on websites like YouTube and other HTML5 video platforms. Instead of being limited to YouTube's built-in 2x maximum, it directly adjusts the underlying video element, allowing you to watch content at speeds well beyond 3x or even 10x if desired. The extension adds a small on-screen controller and customizable keyboard shortcuts so you can instantly speed up, slow down, or reset playback without opening menus. Because it works at the browser level rather than modifying YouTube itself, it's fast, reliable, and popular among students, developers, and fast learners who want a smoother high-speed viewing experience.
